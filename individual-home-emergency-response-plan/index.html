9.

and

shall be familiar with the procedures for calling “911” which are as follows:

  1. Dial “911”.
  2. State: “This is an emergency!”
  3. Give the phone number you are calling from.
  4. Give the address of the emergency.
  5. Describe the problem and how it happened, if known; otherwise, give the facts and describe what has been observed.
  6. Provide your name.
  7. Remain calm.
  8. Follow the “911” dispatcher’s directions.
  9. Advise dispatcher immediately if you are not trained in CPR.
  10. Don’t hang up before the dispatcher hangs up.
  11. Reassure the client/family.
