Incident/Accidents Reporting Acknowledgement
I have been advised by the AFC Provider that I MUST report ALL incidents/accidents and any medical, physical or mental changes in any Member in my care to the RN Care Manager/AFC Provider. I understand that failure to report any incident I become signed may result in disciplinary action by the AFC Provider and that the Provider has a zero tolerance in this activity. I understand that AFC reporting requirements include but are not limited to the following circumstances:
  • Member falls
  • Member injuries
  • Member hospitalizations/emergency department/hospitalization
  • Any changes in Member's physical/mental health affecting the AFC administrative office.
  • Any changes in abuse or neglect.
I understand that all incidents must be reported by AFC staff/caregiver immediately.
Caregiver Ongoing Medication Management
One of the Caregiver's responsibilities is the ongoing task of medication adherence for the Member. The Caregiver will need to fill out the Member's Medication Log for the RN Care Manager and the AFC Provider to review on a monthly basis. If the Member chooses to self-administer their medications, the Caregiver must document this in the Medication Log. The Caregiver is required to outline the reason for any missed medications in the Medication Log.

I understand that the Caregiver must document all medications taken by the Member in a timely manner. If a Member misses a dose for any reason, the Caregiver must document the reason for the missed medication in the Medication Log. I further understand that the Caregiver must notify the RN Care Manager and the AFC Provider of any missed medications. The monthly Medication Log that is obtained at the end of every month.
